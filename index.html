About

The Toyota AE86, also known as the Toyota Corolla Levin and Toyota Sprinter Trueno, is a lightweight (923 kg) hatchback or coupe produced from 1983 to 1987 by Toyota. For brevity, chassis code "AE86" refers to the 1600cc rear wheel drive model.

When the Toyota AE86 was first born, no one expected that the car would become a legend and remain a serious Japanese classic even the day after tomorrow. The Toyota Corolla Levin AE86 was marketed as an affordable, lightweight, and reliable starter car, and was available in both the Japanese market (JDM) and other countries. The uniqueness of the car was in its rear-wheel drive, because by that time most car models had been transferred to front-wheel drive. RWD made the AE86 the perfect machine for enthusiast riders. Engine

The 4A series engines used in the AE86 were also highly regarded for their excellent technical characteristics - long engine life, its simple design and ease of modification. If you delve into the technical details of the model, the Corolla AE86 was equipped with a four-cylinder 1587 cc 4A-GE engine that produces 130 hp. and 150 Nm (JDM version). The same motor was used in the first generation of the Toyota MR2 roadster (AW11). As for the transmission, the AE86 was available with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission, at the request of the buyer, an LSD (limited slip differential) could be installed in the manual transmission.
